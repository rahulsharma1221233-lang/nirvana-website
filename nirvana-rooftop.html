✦ Our Story

A Rooftop Haven
Above Etawah

Perched above the vibrant pulse of Etawah on National Highway 19, Nirvana Rooftop Restro is more than a destination — it's an elevation of the senses. We blend contemporary flavours with warm hospitality, offering an open-sky dining experience unlike any other in Uttar Pradesh.


Whether you arrive for a quiet morning meal or a midnight feast, our team is here around the clock — every day, every hour — to ensure your visit is nothing short of transcendent.
